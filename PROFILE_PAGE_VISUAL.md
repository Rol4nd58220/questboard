## Color Scheme

- **Background**: Dark (#1A1A1A)
- **Cards**: Slightly lighter (#252525)
- **Text Primary**: White (#FFFFFF)
- **Text Secondary**: Gray (#B0B0B0)
- **Accent**: Orange (#FF9800)
- **Buttons**: Orange background with white text

## Interactive Elements

1. **Profile Photo**: Click to view full size (future)
2. **Change Photo Button**: Opens image picker (hidden by default)
3. **Edit Profile**: Opens edit screen
4. **Logout**: Signs out and returns to login

## Data Displayed

- **Name**: From Firestore users/{userId}/fullName
- **Email**: From Firebase Auth
- **Rating**: Calculated from jobCompletions collection
- **Reviews**: Count of employer reviews
- **Jobs Completed**: Total completed jobs
- **About Me**: From users/{userId}/bio or aboutMe
- **Location**: From users/{userId}/location
